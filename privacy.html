Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Axol Assist and the Accessibility Surfer extension are designed to support accessibility features while minimizing data collection.

What We Collect

  • We do not run analytics or tracking scripts on this website.
  • Website contact forms open your local email client; messages are sent by you via your own email provider.
  • The Accessibility Surfer extension stores settings locally in your browser (such as dwell timing and accessibility preferences).

How Extension Data Is Used

  • Settings are used only to apply behavior and UI preferences in your browser.
  • Settings are not sold or shared with third parties.
  • No keystroke logging or remote telemetry is performed by the extension.

Data Storage

  • Extension settings are stored in Chrome extension local storage.
  • You can clear extension data by removing the extension or resetting settings from the UI.

Reading Mode

  • Reading mode extracts and displays article content entirely within your browser.
  • Page content is never transmitted to any server — all processing happens locally.
  • The list of domains where you have used reading mode is stored in Chrome extension local storage and is never shared or transmitted.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to axolassist.business@gmail.com.
