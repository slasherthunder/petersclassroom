Accessibility tools that live on your site.

Most WordPress sites leave visitors with no way to adjust reading settings. AccessFlow installs in under a minute and gives each person persistent controls for typography, contrast, spacing, and motion — aligned with common WCAG usability patterns, not a separate overlay service.

How it works

Four steps. One zip file.

1

Download AccessFlow

Get accessflow.zip from this page. No account or license key.

2

Upload to WordPress

Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin. Select the zip and install.

3

Activate

The toolbar appears on every public page immediately. Optional settings for accent color and position.

4

Visitors adjust

Each person sets font, contrast, spacing, and motion preferences. Settings save in the browser and persist across visits.

Features

Controls visitors actually use.

Font & Text Size

OpenDyslexic plus scaling from 80% to 200%. Helps readers with dyslexia, low vision, and cognitive load.

Contrast & Color

Dark mode, high contrast, grayscale, sepia, and inverted filters applied site-wide in one click.

Spacing Controls

Independent letter spacing, word spacing, and line height for clearer reading on dense pages.

Motion & Reading Aids

Reduce or pause animation, reading guide bar, enhanced focus, link highlighting, and enlarged cursor.
