Who we are

Founder-built, classroom-tested.

Axol Assist was founded by a classroom educator frustrated by how expensive and hard to access assistive technology is for the students who need it most. Every product is built in close collaboration with parents, students, SLPs, and AT specialists — the people who live this work every day. We believe great accessibility tools should be affordable enough for any family and any school.
